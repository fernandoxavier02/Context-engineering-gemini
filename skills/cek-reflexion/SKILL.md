# Reflexion Skill

When this skill is activated, you must:

1. **Pause and Review**: Read the entire preceding turn and the user's last request.
2. **Identify Failures**: List at least 3 potential edge cases, failures, or simplifications in your proposed logic or implementation.
3. **Propose Correction**: Provide a detailed corrected approach based on the reflection findings.
4. **⚠️ STOP AND ASK**: After your reflection, you MUST NOT proceed with the implementation. You must ask the user: "Deseja que eu prossiga com esta estratégia corrigida ou prefere ajustar algo?"

Only continue after explicit user confirmation.
